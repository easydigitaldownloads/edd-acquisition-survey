Collect information from customers about how they found your site.

== Description ==

How did you hear about us? Acquisition Survey for Easy Digital Downloads adds a drop down of customizable options to ask your customers how they heard about you upon checkout. Get detailed statistics about how many orders and the total earnings from the methods in the EDD Reports section.
